# Nexara Content Architecture Audit

## Core Position

Nexara must stay organized around three equal sections:

- Academy: capability in people.
- Digital Marketing: capability in markets.
- Labs: capability in systems.

The gateway changes presentation, not company structure. Neo uses high-energy language for builders and fast-moving teams. Trust uses professional language for institutions, enterprise buyers, and decision-makers.

## Current Gaps Addressed

- Stack labels need explanations, deliverables, proof and next actions.
- Section pages need audience fit, process and packages.
- Subpages need stronger detail instead of single-line cards.
- Customer proof needs clearer context and outcomes.
- CTAs need section-specific intake paths.
- FAQs need to reduce decision friction.
- Incorporated-company posture needs facts, standards and an enquiry route.
- Unverified metrics, fake client names and internal drafting notes must not ship.

## Page Architecture

Home:
- Hero with the three-section proposition.
- Equal-weight section cards.
- Callout explaining gateway logic.
- Stack matrix with detailed capabilities.
- Customer proof rail.

Section Pages:
- Hero with theme-specific language.
- Subpage navigation.
- Audience fit cards.
- Detailed modules.
- Stack detail grid.
- Process timeline.
- Package cards.
- Proof cards.
- FAQ and intake CTA.

Subpages:
- Focused callout.
- Detailed cards with outcomes and deliverables.
- Same proof, FAQ and intake ladder.

Customers:
- Readiness proof by section until approved client stories are available.
- Theme-aware proof language.

Company:
- Incorporated-company facts, ideology, principles and operating standards.

Contact:
- Section-aware briefing route for Academy, Digital Marketing, Labs and combined plays.
- Checklist of information needed before Nexara can scope the next step.

## Production Content Decisions

- Replace unverifiable metrics with operating facts.
- Replace fake customer names with delivery-model proof.
- Keep the `customers` route for backwards compatibility, but present it as proof/readiness rather than invented case studies.
- Route all intake CTAs to `/#theme/contact/:section`.
- Keep incorporation facts generic until the legal name, CIN or LLPIN, address, email, phone and GST details are approved for publication.

## Required Future Inputs

To replace all representative content with real business content, collect:

- Actual course names, durations, fees and placement policy.
- Digital Marketing service packages, deliverables and example timelines.
- Labs product status, security posture, deployment options and demos.
- Real client names or anonymized case studies.
- Preferred enquiry channels: form, WhatsApp, phone, email or booking link.
- Final numbers for metrics and claims.
- Public legal identity details: registered entity name, CIN or LLPIN, registered office, contact email, phone and GST status if applicable.
